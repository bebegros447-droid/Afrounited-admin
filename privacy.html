Afrounited Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Afrounited. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our mobile applications (Rider and Driver apps) and services.

2. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Name, phone number, email address, and official identification documents (for driver verification).
  • Location Data: We collect precise background location data from drivers to enable ride tracking and dispatch. We collect temporary location data from riders to establish pickup points.
  • Financial Information: Mobile money numbers (e.g., Orange Money, Wave) for processing payouts.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data to facilitate transportation and delivery logistics, process payments securely, verify driver identity for platform safety, and provide customer support.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We only share necessary information (like a driver's name and vehicle model) with the paired rider/customer to facilitate the service.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy or your data, please contact our support team at the official Afrounited headquarters.
